 Once your account is created, sign in to the AWS Management Console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/.
In the search bar, type "Billing" and select the Billing dashboard.
In the left menu, click "Billing preferences" and enable "Receive Free Tier Usage Alerts." Save your preferences.
(Optional) Set up a billing alarm: In the search bar, type "CloudWatch" and go to the CloudWatch service. Click "Alarms" > "Create alarm." Follow the prompts to set an alarm for the "EstimatedCharges" metric.
Explore the AWS Console: Familiarize yourself with the navigation bar, services menu, and account settings.
